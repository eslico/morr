Why do women do this?.

The question of treason has been standing for many years. And men are often concerned about the reasons that encourage women to go for it. Treason is like a betrayal of your loved one. And if a man who cheats is driven by lust and the desire to feel new feelings, then women have it much more complicated. They have there may be 1000 reasons in your head why they want to do this. Today we will consider them exactly:

Feels undervalued

Most women live by their emotions. And they like it when a man compliments them, takes care of them. Then they feel attractive and desirable to their partner men. But once these feelings evaporate, they go in search of a new partner who will close this need for them.

Bad attitude

When a man begins to forget about relationships and stops valuing his woman, then her departure can be instantaneous. Or she will first cheat on him, and then already escapes. Although there are cases when, on the contrary, girls like it when they are treated poorly, but this is another story.

She stopped loving me

As sad as it may sound, but in any relationship, there can come a point when a woman no longer feels anything for her man. But this is not a reason to change. It is better to put all the dots over the "and"at once. And part peacefully and calmly, not deceiving each other, going to the left.

She doesn't respect her man

Infidelities often occur in couples where the dominant position in the relationship is occupied by a woman. Then she has nothing to change to a man. She does not feel the power in him and looks for it on the side. And even worse, that the man even finds out about it, forgives her for infidelity. Than even lower falls in the eyes of a woman.

She's drunk and she's taking revenge

Alcohol, of course, contributes to infidelity. After all, as we all know, what a drunk girl does not mistress. But that's not a good reason to forgive her. There are also cases when a girl specifically wants to take revenge, for this purpose getting drunk and giving herself to the first person she meets.

You're not listening to her

For a woman, a man is primarily her mainstay and best friend.
With whom, she can share a man with innermost secrets and on whom he can rely at any moment. And if she stops seeing it in you, then she will hurry to find a guy who will listen and comfort her.

Met someone else

Unfortunately, this also happens. Basically, when you've been living together for a long time and the relationship has turned into a everyday life and routine.
And if you notice that the girl has become somewhat secretive and her behavior has changed dramatically, then she has someone on the side. Do not close your eyes to this, and think that you are doing well. It was better to talk to her and decide everything at once.
