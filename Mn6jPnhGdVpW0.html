How not to screw up when Dating a girl?

In fact, women always evaluate men. Always.

They decidewhether to give a man a chance, even before he comes to meet her.

For example, if you behave in public bad: if you are rude to the waiters, if you use rude language, then the chances of convincing her that you are a good guy are very small.
⠀
Girls, usually, approach this issue with great caution. They need confidence that you are a great and adequate guy, and will not cause them any harm harm's.

Therefore, most often a woman twists phrases such as: "Why should I turn him off?» or "What doesn't suit me about him anymore?" Which, of course, hinders not only men, but also women too.
⠀
Ask her how to change her mind.
⠀
For it is necessary to show the girl its adequacy. Certainly not to sit quietly on the sidelines with a stone face, looking at her. This behavior will not be interesting to anyone.

Therefore, it is necessary to behave differently than everyone else. You need to be able to stand out, to be special, not like others. You don't have to be able to walking barefoot on broken glass or talking in 5 languages.

What matters is your image, which you must constantly work on.
⠀
First, don't act like an impulsive or harsh person. This behavior only scares off the entire female sex. Sudden movements betray you uncertainty and fear. And a woman feels this very well, and will never date an insecure man. On the contrary, cultivate calmness, smoothness, and confidence in your movements.
⠀
Secondly, do not confuse the image with appearance and expensive clothing. Of course, clothes give a person their own style. charm. If it has taste. But still, it should only be as a complement, and not as the basis of your image. It is enough just to be well-groomed, clean and tidy.

Third, personal comfort is a necessary rule. You should feel comfortable in any situation. Many people say: "Be happy", to be yourself, means to feel good in your own body. We feel uncomfortable with those people who constantly feel "out of place", those who deceive the environment in order to appear better and cooler.

I will say right away, in our time, any deception is revealed quite quickly, and in the future most of the time, you can't be what you're not. And at worst, ruin your reputation.

Therefore, the only way out is to develop new skills, develop cool habits and constantly work on yourself.

Fourth, girls still pay attention to your surroundings and they draw certain conclusions. But this is also a plus, if there are other girls in your company. This automatically removes many of the questions and fears in the girl's head.

In addition, if a girl sees your demand for the opposite sex, then in her head, she will begin to ask questions: "Why is he doing this they are attracted to you?", "What is so special about him?" while finding only positive qualities in you.

Therefore, it is important to work on your image, constantly improving yourself.
