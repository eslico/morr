Two main components of communication on the Internet

Have you ever wondered what to write on the Internet or what to reply to this or that message? I don't think so. In our time, social networks have almost replaced real communication, and it has become commonplace for us.

Although if remember, before for every SMS, you had to pay real money.

My friends constantly asked me what to say to a girl in this or that situation, and I decided to reveal this topic here. I think you are also interested in this question.

Almost always, for a cool interaction, you need 2 things: comfort and attraction.

Comfort, plays an important component, because if it does not exist, then the person will not trust you, and feel uncomfortable. Of course, it is much more difficult to build it over the Internet than at a personal meeting, but it is real.

Attraction is formed through play, out of the reach of man. Imagine a situation where cats run after a dot on the wall. You need to interest the object and give it hope that it will succeed.

Attraction and comfort are the most important things to start a relationship with. If there is no comfort, then your affair will end as quickly as it did and it started. And if there is no attraction, then at best you will just become friends.

Some people claimthat to start Dating, you just need to be a normal guy, girl and communicate well. There is one "but" here, if there is no game, then you will appear to your partner as a great conversationalist and a human being, without sexual attraction to you. That will not allow you to achieve what you want.

All this is not visible on the Internet, we do not feel each other. And if you don't create the right image in your partner's head with the help of correspondence, then you will remain just friends.

Let's talk about creating comfort:

The most important rule is to communicate with people as if you have already known them for a long time.

I have a friend who quickly and easily finds a common language with any person.

Its main feature is that it does not share borders. He comes up and introduces himself as if this is their 100th meeting, or as if they've known each other for a long time.

The main thing is that it always works, and he quickly gets in touch and makes a good impression on himself.
⠀
Therefore, from the very beginning of correspondence, try to communicate as if you are close to each other and have known each other for a long time.

For Example: A Girl: «Hi». Guy: "hi, I was just thinking about you."It is clear that no one will respond in this way to a complete stranger, so you create some kind of contact between you.

But communicating in this style can easily screw up and make a number of mistakes.
